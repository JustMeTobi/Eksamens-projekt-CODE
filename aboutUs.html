OUR STORY

Discover Libra Classic, a cozy tattoo haven nestled in the vibrant Latin Quarter of Aarhus. Within our rustic, half-timbered abode, skilled artists from around the globe buzzing needles, each with their own unique style.

With a commitment to excellence, we craft high-quality, original designs spanning Fineline, Tribal, Traditional, Ornamental, Neo-Traditional, and more. Our meticulous attention to detail ensures every tattoo is a masterpiece.

From booking to the final reveal, we prioritize transparency and seamless service. Consultations, feedback, and boundless creativity await, making every project, big or small, an unforgettable journey at Libra Classic.
